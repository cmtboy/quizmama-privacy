Last Updated: September 6, 2023

Information We Collect

We collect your data for the following purposes:

We utilize the following third-party services:

User Consent

By providing your username and Gmail address, you consent to the collection and storage of this information for the purposes stated above.

Data Storage

Your data is securely stored on Firebase, a trusted backend provided by Google.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. Your information is kept strictly confidential.

We will notify you of any updates to our app and this privacy policy through the email address you provided.
